About Us

SR Consulting offers the comprehensive capabilities and deep industry knowledge necessary to help you navigate the complexity of pharmaceutical product development. Dr. Samer Rajput has experience in developing different types of pharmaceutical products in multiple therapeutic areas, and in all stages of product development, from translational research through post marketing studies.​​ ​ Dr. Samer Rajput is a board certified neurologist with additional credentials and fellowship training in both neuro-immunology and neuro-oncology. Dr. Samer Rajput has held academic positions, managed clinical practices, and served as investigator on numerous clinical trials before joining the pharmaceutical industry. Dr. Samer Rajput has helped many companies achieve their goals over the years. ​ We bring a unique blend of strategic thinking and over 30 years of experience to help sponsors tackle the complexity of pharmaceutical product development.
